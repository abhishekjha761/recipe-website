Paneer Tikka

Home Pasta Pizza Rajma Dal Makhani Queries

Ingredients

  • Paneer cubes
  • Curd (yogurt)
  • Capsicum & Onion
  • Spices (red chili, turmeric, garam masala)
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Mix curd and spices to make marinade
  2. Add paneer and vegetables
  3. Marinate for 30 minutes
  4. Grill or cook on pan

Preparation Time: 40 mins

Difficulty: Medium
